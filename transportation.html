Getting Around Taniti

Convenient transportation to explore every corner of the island.

How to Get Around

  • Rental Cars: Convenient for exploring the island at your own pace.
  • Buses: Affordable routes that reach major attractions and towns.
  • Bikes & Scooters: Eco-friendly and perfect for short rides.
  • Ferries: Connect Taniti with its charming smaller islets.
