Getting Around Taniti

Convenient transportation to explore every corner of the island.

Getting to the Island

By Air

Taniti is served by a small airport that can accommodate small jets and propeller planes. Taniti is in the process of expanding the airport so larger jets will be able to land on the island within the next few years.

By Sea

A small cruise ships regularly visit Taniti docking in Yellow Leaf Bay for one night per week.

Getting Around the Island

Buses

Public buses serve Taniti City and run from 5 a.m. to 11 p.m. every day. Private buses serve the rest of the island.

Taxis

Taxis are available in Taniti City and can be booked for personal tours or short rides.

Rental Cars

Car rentals offer full freedom to explore the island. Rental cars can be booked at a local rental agency near the airport.
