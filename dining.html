Dining in Taniti

Whether you're craving freshly caught seafood, international flavors, or everyday essentials, Taniti offers a wide range of dining and shopping options to satisfy every taste.

Restaurants

Local Fish

Enjoy Taniti’s famous freshly caught fish dishes — grilled, steamed, or prepared island-style with tropical spices.

American

Find comforting favorites from burgers and fries to family-friendly diners, all with an island twist.

Pan-Asian

Experience the vibrant fusion of Asian flavors — from Japanese sushi to Thai curries and Hawaiian poke bowls.

Grocery Stores

Supermarkets

Stock up on fresh produce, snacks, and local ingredients at Taniti’s full-service supermarkets.

Local Grocery Stores

Support small island grocers offering organic produce, tropical fruits, and traditional Tanitian staples.

Convenience Store

Quickly grab essentials, snacks, or drinks at the island's only convenience store, Tikimart Express. Open 24hrs.
