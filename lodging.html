Relax in Taniti’s Finest Lodging

From luxury resorts to seaside bungalows, comfort awaits you.

Accommodations for Every Traveler

Taniti has a wide variety of lodging that ranges from an inexpensive hostel to one large, four-star resort. There are many small, family-owned hotels and a growing number of bed and breakfasts. All types of lodging are strictly regulated and regularly inspected by the Tanitian government.

Resorts

Enjoy full-service luxury at Taniti’s seaside resorts. Relax by infinity pools, savor fine dining, and soak in breathtaking ocean views.

Bed & Breakfasts

Experience Taniti’s hospitality in charming bed and breakfasts offering homemade meals and authentic island comfort.

Private Vacation Rentals

Find your own tropical retreat with private rentals perfect for families, groups, or romantic getaways — comfort and privacy combined.

Hostels

Stay at the island’s friendly hostels, where budget travelers can meet fellow adventurers and enjoy a vibrant, community-focused atmosphere.
